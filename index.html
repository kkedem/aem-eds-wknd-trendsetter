Frequently asked questions

How do you identify what's actually trending?

Our team of style scouts monitors street culture across major cities—New York, London, Tokyo, Lagos, and beyond. We combine on-the-ground reporting with social listening tools to separate genuine trend emergence from manufactured hype.

We never rely on brands to tell us what's trending—we go directly to the community.

Can I share my own style for the community to see?

Absolutely. We love seeing real looks from real people. Tag your photos with #WKNDTrendsetters on Instagram or TikTok and our editorial team reviews community submissions weekly.

Outstanding submissions get featured in our Style Gallery and may be invited to contribute a full editorial piece.

Where do you find your style inspiration?

Everywhere. Skate parks, music festivals, college campuses, night markets, sports arenas—style doesn't live in fashion weeks alone. Our editorial team draws from music, sport, art, and subcultures that rarely get mainstream coverage.

How can I join the WKND Trendsetters community?

Subscribe to our newsletter to get the freshest content delivered weekly. You can also follow us on Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest for daily style drops.

For deeper involvement—contributing, collaborating, or brand partnerships—reach out via our contact page.
